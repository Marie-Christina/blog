Clinic Overview

Location: Gangnam, Seoul (Sinsa Station).

Clinic Type: Aesthetic clinic.

Atmosphere: Upscale, comfortable, and not crowded.

Foreigner Experience and Consultation

This was very comfortable as a non-Korean speaker. The clinic runs separate language floors (English, Korean, and Japanese), and communication felt clear from booking through treatment. Consultation was doctor-led and included a skin analysis scan, then treatment recommendations based on measured concerns.

Staff English ability: Fluent.

Booking ease: Very easy via WhatsApp with fast communication.

Upselling level: Mild and optional; no pressure to add extras.

Plan personalization: High, with scan-based recommendations and patient preference respected.

Treatments and Results

Devices available: Extensive options with major machines and package combinations.

What I tried: Potenza, Inmode, Coretox, Aquapeel, and laser removal of a collapsed pore-related bump.

Why recommended: Inmode for jawline tightening, Potenza for pores/fine lines, Coretox for forehead/11s/frown correction, plus targeted laser for the bump.

Results were strong: smoother texture, smaller pores, and improved bounce. Inmode lift became more noticeable with time, and Coretox placement looked natural. The pore-related bump improved significantly; treatment stayed conservative to reduce scarring risk.

This was a strong first clinic experience overall, and the combination of treatments made skin look clearer and healthier for an extended period.

Downtime and side effects: Mild redness; Coretox settled over about a week; no major side effects.

Was it worth it? Yes.

Pricing, Logistics, and Safety

Cost: About $800.

Compared to other clinics: More expensive.

Hidden costs or foreigner tax: None observed; specials were clearly offered and VAT process was straightforward.

How I booked: WhatsApp.

Ease of finding clinic: Easy, though the different language branches can be confusing at first.

Wait times: None on this visit.

Payment options: Foreign card accepted.

Who performed procedures: Doctor for Inmode, Coretox, Potenza, and laser; technician for Aquapeel.

Visible safety measures: New tip shown, gloves and masks used, and clean clinical environment.

Aftercare instructions: Clear and detailed, including cream usage guidance and scar-minimization advice.

Best For

Ideal for: First-timers, travelers wanting full doctor consultation, and those prioritizing tailored planning.

Not great for: Budget-focused travelers or people who already know exactly what they want and prefer in-and-out visits.

Final Verdict and Closing Notes

Would I go back? Yes, especially when I want a deeper consultation or periodic skin check over time. This clinic costs more, but the quality of consultation and aftercare attention is excellent.

If your priority is a thoughtful, doctor-led plan with strong communication, this is a great entry point into Korean skin clinics.
