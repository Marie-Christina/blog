Clinic Overview

Location: Songdo, Incheon. About 30 minutes from the airport by taxi and 40-50 minutes by bus/subway.

Clinic Type: Aesthetic clinic.

Atmosphere: Casual, quiet, and not crowded.

Foreigner Experience and Consultation

Booking was easy over WhatsApp and the clinic flow was smooth. Doctors and the clinic coordinator were fluent in English, while front desk, sales, and technicians used basic English with translator support when needed.

Staff English ability: Basic at support level, fluent with doctor and coordinator.

Booking ease: Very easy through WhatsApp.

Comfort level: Foreigner-friendly with practical translator support.

Consultation quality: Doctor-led, no pushy upselling, and treatment options were explained clearly.

Treatments and Results

Devices available: Pico, Clarity, Ulthera, and more.

What I tried: Secret Laser, Botox, Aquapeel, and mole removal.

Why recommended: These matched my goals. I usually choose Potenza, but this location offered Secret Laser as an alternative.

One unusual note: with Secret Laser, I could hear and feel a distinct needling sensation that I did not notice with Potenza. It was still manageable after numbing cream.

Results noticed: Early improvement in texture and overall brightness.

Downtime and side effects: Temporary redness and a brief flush later; no major downtime.

Was it worth it? Yes.

Pricing, Logistics, and Safety

Cost: Approximately $350 after VAT refund.

Compared to other clinics: Similar overall pricing.

Hidden costs or foreigner tax: None. Korean-site pricing matched what I paid, with VAT handled through tax-free processing.

How I booked: WhatsApp.

Ease of finding clinic: A little tricky at first. From the Naver pin, go up the stairs to the third floor and head right down the hallway.

Wait times: None. Very punctual.

Payment options: Foreign card worked with no issues.

Extra note: Branding looked separate from other Shinebeam channels, but this is the same Shinebeam group.

Who performed procedures: Doctor performed Erbium laser, botox, and Secret Laser. Technician handled numbing, cooling care, and Aquapeel.

Visible safety measures: Yes. New tips, glove changes, masks, and clean environment.

Aftercare instructions: Minimal, likely because my treatment profile was familiar.

Best For

Ideal for: ICN layovers and travelers staying in Incheon.

Not great for: First-timers unsure what concerns to target, or people uncomfortable using translation apps.

Final Verdict and Closing Notes

Would I go back? Yes. I would return, especially for a 24-hour layover trip. The staff was friendly, doctors were honest and patient, and results were strong.

I would love to see easier airport transport for this location in the future, but overall this was a positive experience.

At the end of the day, no clinic is perfect, but some are clearly better suited to travelers, first-timers, or people chasing specific results. My goal is to share practical insight that helps you spend less time second-guessing and more time getting the treatments you actually want.
